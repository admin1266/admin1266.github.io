Acid base and salts

Acids and Bases are used in everyday life in many ways.They play a role in digestion of food we eat, medicines we take and in cleaning products we use.

Acids

Many food items such as Vinegar and lemon juice taste sour because they contain acid.

Types of acids

Organic acids
They occur naturally in plants and animals.They contain carbon and hydrogen atoms and they are weak acids.
Example: Lactic acid, Citric acid
Inorganic acids
These acids derive from mineral resources. They contain hydrogen but no carbon atom. Most of them are very Strong
Example: Sulphuric Acid H2SO4.
